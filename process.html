Statistical Consultation Process

Stage 1 – Submission of Research Proposal Prior to Defense

Before the proposal defense, students are required to submit their thesis/dissertation proposals for checking. They shall be given an initial clearance which will serve as a basis that the proposal has been approved by the Center as to the following criteria:

  1. Correctness of the statistical design;
  2. Validity and reliability of the instrument;
  3. Appropriateness of the statistical tool related to the objectives of study and the type of research; and
  4. Adequacy of the sample size.

Stage 2 – Submission of Research Proposal After the Defense
After the proposal defense, students are required to submit their thesis/dissertation proposals for rechecking. They shall be given a clearance which will serve as a basis that the Center has given them permission to proceed on gathering their data. This includes validation of research instrument, pilot testing, and gathering data to target samples. (Request for Clearance of Conduct Research)

Stage 1 and 2 will be covered by Service 1(Initial Consultation) of the Center.

Stage 3 – Submission of Data All gathered data are required to be submitted to the Center. Data encoding, checking, validation, and cleaning will be carried out in this stage. In addition, all instruments used during the data collection process should be properly submitted by the researcher to the Center for verification.

Service 2 (Data Encoding/Cleaning) will be covered on this stage.

Stage 4 – Data Analysis and Interpretation Once the data are collected and passed in stage two (2), it will be properly and appropriately be analyzed by the statisticians of the Center. Statistician will be responsible for checking the conclusions, suggesting the best ways to describe and display the data, and basic statistical interpretation of the findings. All outputs/results will be formatted using the standard format and will be interpreted to the client.

Either Service 3 (Analysis/Interpretation) or Service 4 (Output Checking) will be covered on this stage. In the case that the student has availed service 3, service 4 will no longer be necessary. On other hand, all statistical analysis that was not done by the Center are required to avail service 4 (Output Checking) of the Center.

Stage 5 – Clearance from the CenterThe clearance from the Center includes the initial and final clearance. The intial clearance shall be issued to the researcher if the proposal was already approved for defense. The final clearance shall be issued if the research methods applied and basic interpretations are already checked and approved for final defense. The charge for a consultation will depend on the service availed by the student. For detailed breakdown of fees, see Chapter V, section III of this manual.

Schedule of payment – Fees will be collected once the student availed the service of the Center for Statistics and Computing Sciences. Additional payment will be charged to the student depending on the consultation services. Additional charge should be settled before the release of the results/outputs. All payment shall be properly settled at the Cashiers Office in their respective campus.

All students conducting thesis/dissertation for the graduate and undergraduate level are required to secure a final clearance from the center.
